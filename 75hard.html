Nutrition
Diet Plan
Clean whole foods. High protein. Dairy is fine. Most of this is Pick 'n Save or Aldi friendly.
The Approach: High protein, real food, no processed junk, no alcohol. Full dairy is on the table. Everything on this plan is available at Pick 'n Save or Aldi — no specialty stores needed.

Recovery
Stretch & Yoga
Morning 10 min + Evening 10 min daily. Full 45-min session Wed & Sun. Each routine has a guided timed player.
Dan's rules: No deep forward folds under load. No overpressure on lumbar flexion. Cat-cow daily. Pigeon = supine version only. Stop any stretch that sends pain down the leg.

Morning Routine · 10 min · Daily

6 STRETCHES · TIMED HOLDS · AUTO-ADVANCE
Morning Wake-Up Flow
10 min · reference
▼
1
Cat-Cow · 10 reps · 90 sec — Hands and knees. Inhale to arch (cow), exhale to round (cat). Dan's #1 disc exercise. Non-negotiable every morning.
2
Child's Pose · 60 sec — Sit back on heels, arms forward. Dan: widen knees if lower back feels compressed.
3
Thread the Needle · 30 sec each side — From all-fours, slide one arm under body. Thoracic rotation without lumbar load.
4
Supine Knee Hug · 30 sec each + both — On back, hug one knee to chest, then both. Lumbar decompression. Safe for bulged discs.
5
Pelvic Tilts · 15 reps — On back, knees bent. Flatten lower back to floor then release. Lumbar stability. Back-safe ✓
6
Hip Flexor Stretch · 45 sec each side — Half-kneeling, lean forward. Tight hip flexors pull on the lumbar spine — critical for Dan.

Evening Routine · 10 min · Daily

5 STRETCHES · TIMED HOLDS · AUTO-ADVANCE
Evening Wind-Down
10 min · reference
▼
1
Legs Up the Wall · 3 min — On back, legs up a wall. Decompresses lower back, reverses leg blood pooling. Watch TV like this.
2
Supine Twist · 45 sec each side — One knee across body, look opposite. Dan: keep both shoulders flat.
3
Band Hamstring Stretch · 45 sec each leg — Loop resistance band around foot, lift leg while lying on back. Safe for lumbar.
4
Figure-4 Glute Stretch · 60 sec each side — Ankle over opposite knee, pull bottom leg toward chest. Tight glutes and piriformis contribute to sciatic irritation.
5
Diaphragmatic Breathing · 2 min — Hand on belly. 4 counts in, 2 hold, 6 out. Best free recovery tool you're not using.

Full Yoga Flow · 45 min · Wed & Sun

10 POSES · TIMED HOLDS · AUTO-ADVANCE
Warm-Up · 10 min
10 min
▼
1
Cat-Cow · 2 min — 10 slow reps to open the session.
2
Downward Dog · 60 sec × 2 — Dan: bend knees, lengthen spine over straightening legs. Walk the dog.
3
Low Lunge (Crescent) · 60 sec each side — Front knee over ankle, back knee down, arms overhead.
4
World's Greatest Stretch · 45 sec each side — From lunge, elbow to floor, rotate arm to sky. Hits hip, thoracic, and hamstring together.
Standing Flow · 15 min
15 min
▼
1
Mountain → Forward Fold → Half-Lift · 5 reps — Dan: slight knee bend on fold, hands to knees not floor.
2
Warrior I · 60 sec each side — Front knee bent, hips square forward, arms overhead.
3
Warrior II · 60 sec each side — Hips open, arms extended. Hold and breathe through the burn.
4
Triangle Pose · 45 sec each side — Dan: hand to shin or knee only. No aggressive lateral bend.
5
Chair Pose · 45 sec × 3 — Feet together, sit back, arms overhead. Back stays long.
Floor Flow + Savasana · 20 min
20 min
▼
1
Supine Pigeon / Pigeon · 90 sec each side — Dan: supine (figure-4 on back). Carrie: floor pigeon. Most important hip stretch for rowing and squatting.
2
Seated Spinal Twist · 60 sec each side — Dan: go only as far as comfortable.
3
Band Hamstring Stretch · 60 sec each leg — On back, band around foot, lift leg.
4
Bridge Pose · 10 reps + 30 sec hold — Drive hips to ceiling. Strengthens glutes and lumbar extensors. Back-safe ✓
5
Happy Baby · 60 sec — On back, grab outer feet, knees wide. Pure decompression after a row session.
6
Legs Up the Wall · 3 min — Non-negotiable finish.
7
Savasana · 5 min — Flat on back, eyes closed, completely still. Set a timer. Stay the full 5 minutes.
